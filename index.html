I. The Commercial Opportunity

Nature in Nepal is a Competitive Advantage.

Nepal has globally significant mega-fauna but no premium domestic wildlife content. **Wild Nepal** bridges the gap. It provides the visual splendor of *David Attenborough* but anchors it in the visceral, heartbeat-driven tension of *Man vs. Wild*.
