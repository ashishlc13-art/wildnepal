I. Narrative Tone Strategy

The Three-Voice Formula

To compete with international streamers (BBC/Netflix), we employ a rotating tonal structure that ensures viewer retention across 40 minutes.

01. Wade Mode (The Mystery)

Detective-style investigation. Is the species endangered? Endangered? Behaving strangely? High stakes, uncertainty.

02. Grylls Mode (Field Grit)

Visceral immersion. The host is wet, cold, tracking through thick grass. Audience feels the environment physically.

03. Attenborough Mode (Revelation)

Cinematic wonder. Slow-motion 4K. Minimal narration. The camera lets the animal's life exist on its own terms.

Cast Archetype: The Conservation Journalist

Recommended host must be the audience's eyes/body in the field at all times.
